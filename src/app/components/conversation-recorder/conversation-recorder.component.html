How it works
  • Click "Start Recording" to begin capturing audio
  • Audio is continuously recorded in 10-second segments
  • Each segment is automatically sent to the backend
  • Suggestions are generated and displayed in real-time
  • Previous suggestions are stored in the accordion
  • Click "Stop Recording" to end the session
